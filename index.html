How they filmed Venom

The visual effects team brought in a tall extra who was about two meters tall. He wore a helmet with a ball attached to the top of his head. It was this ball that the actors had to look at as they interacted with Venom, a place where the eyes of the creature were. "The statue work gave an idea of the space the character would occupy on set," Franklin says. - That helped the camera crew, the actors and the set designers. When we inserted the computer character during editing, it fit the scene perfectly."
