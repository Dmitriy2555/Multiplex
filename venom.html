Venom

Year: 2021 Rating: 9.3 Director: Ruben Fleischer

Description

More than a year after those events, journalist Eddie Brock tries to adjust to life as the host of an alien symbiote, Venom, who endows him with superhuman abilities. Brock tries to revive his career and interviews serial killer Cletus Cassady, who happens to be the host of Carnage and escapes from prison after a botched execution. The film will continue the story of Eddie Brock becoming Venom. The new part will focus on the confrontation between Venom (Tom Hardy) and Cletus Cassidy (Woody Harrelson). The original premiere date was scheduled for Oct. 2, 2020, but it was later postponed due to the coronavirus pandemic.
