Focus

Year: 2015 Rating: 9.7 Director: John Recua

Description

The story of an experienced con man who falls in love with a girl who takes her first steps in the field of illegal money-laundering from citizens. The relationship becomes a problem for them when it is discovered that romance interferes with their dishonest business. At the center of the events of the movie "Focus" is an experienced con artist Nicky Spurgeon, known as the Zephyr. One day he meets Jess Barrett, who has just begun her journey as a con artist. Spurgeon becomes her teacher, accepts her into his team, participates with her in several scams, and then falls in love with her. A romance flares up between them, but it soon turns out that this relationship is a problem for their criminal activities...
