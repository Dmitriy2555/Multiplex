Sharp Visors

The gangster television saga "Sharp Visors" takes the viewer back to the early twentieth century. At the center of the plot of "Sharp Visors" is a young gypsy gang from Birmingham. It is led by the Shelby brothers, who have barely returned from World War I, which brutalized many. The gang gains influence, plundering and gambling, intimidating and inspiring respect for ordinary townsfolk. For confrontations they use an original weapon - a blade sewn into the visor of a cap. Thomas Shelby is the true ringleader of the gang, even though publicly he is the oldest of the four brothers, Arthur. Tough and pushy, he's willing to do anything to extend his family's influence and gain a foothold in this harsh postwar world.

Mechanics

Arthur Bishop is the Mechanic, a highly professional, impeccable hitman who always works by the rules - cleanly and without trace. Such work requires complete composure and impartiality, and in his work he has no equal. Bishop has always worked alone, but he had to become a mentor to the young and desperate Steve. The methodical professional and the impulsive apprentice now pair up to fix problems, but sooner or later they will come together in a deadly battle. In The Mechanic, we learn the story of Arthur Bishop, a professional hitman who always works flawlessly and has no equal. One day his close friend Harry is killed, and Arthur has to become a mentor for his son Steve, who also dreams of revenge. Now the two of them solve problems together, but soon become a problem for someone themselves.

Witcher

The protagonist of the series is the witch Heralt, a hunter of dangerous monsters that threaten the human race. His strength comes from the mutations he underwent as a child: Geralt has an accelerated metabolism, fast reactions, and inhuman endurance. The official synopsis of the series focuses on the story of Geralt as he tries to find his place in a world where humans are often more evil than beasts. The Witcher Geralt, mutant and monster killer, travels the Continent on his trusty horse, named Plotva.
