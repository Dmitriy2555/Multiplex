Bad Boys

Year: 2021 Rating: 9.9 Director: Mike Bey

Description

Detectives Mike Lowry and Marcus Burnett are at it again! True, they are suspended from all operations, but has anything ever stopped the desperate friends? After all, this time Mike is being hunted by someone from his past life. So the guys have to go all-out! Burnett quarreled with his partner, left the Miami Police Department, and took up private detective work. Lowry, meanwhile, is going through a midlife crisis and is thinking of giving up the bachelor lifestyle. At the same time, he has to deal with a cheeky new partner. The "bad guys" have to team up again when an Albanian mercenary who wants to avenge his brother's death begins to hunt for them. In order to survive, the former partners must let go of old grudges.
